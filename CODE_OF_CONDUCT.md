# Code of conduct

## Our standard

ScrapeStudio welcomes constructive participation from people of every background and experience level. Be respectful, discuss ideas rather than individuals, assume good intent while accepting correction, and make technical feedback specific and actionable.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate disruption, publishing private information, or encouraging abusive scraping behavior are not acceptable.

## Project boundaries

Contributions must respect the product's safety and responsible-use rules. Do not use project spaces to request credential theft, CAPTCHA or anti-bot bypass, private-network access, proxy rotation, unauthorized collection, or publication of sensitive data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate this standard and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Reports should be made privately through the repository's GitHub security advisory or the maintainer profile linked in the README. Reports will be reviewed promptly, discreetly, and without retaliation against good-faith reporters.

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other spaces where someone represents ScrapeStudio.
